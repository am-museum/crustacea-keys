Australomysis incisa (Sars, 1883)

Mysidopsis incisa Sars, 1883: 40. --Sars, 1885: 202, figs 21–23.
Australomysis incisa. --Tattersall, 1927: 249, fig. 101. --Dakin & Colefax, 1940: 134.--Gordan, 1957: 341. --Mauchline & Murano, 1977: 49. --Mauchline, 1980: 332. --Fenton, 1985c: 88, 90 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 451.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: whereabouts unknown (lost).

Type locality. Entrance of Port Phillip Bay, Victoria, Australia.

Description. Based on Sars, 1885: 202, figs 21–23.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, narrowly rounded; spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale ratio of length x width 2:1 to 5:1, longer than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins with setae, spines on lateral margins absent, spines on medial margin absent, distally rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present.
Pereon. Pleopod 3 terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, robust setae from statocyst nearly to the apex, slender setae present, endopod and exopod subequal in length. Uropod exopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae on lateral margin present, slender setae on lateral margin on entire margin. Telson longer than broad, distally truncate, cleft or notched, cleftness less than 20%, without plumose setae, robust setae on inner margin present, robust setae along entire inner margin, robust setae on lateral margins present, on entire lateral margins with a median gap.

Habitat. Continental shelf, sublittoral.

Depth zones. Littoral.

Australian distribution. New South Wales: off Broken Bay (Dakin & Colefax, 1940). Victoria: Port Phillip Bay (Sars, 1883). Bass Strait: (Fenton, 1985c). Tasmania: surf beach, Morina Bay (Fenton, 1985c). South Australia: Vivonne Bay, south coast of Kangaroo Island (Tattersall, 1927); Thistle Island (Fenton, 1985c).

Australian geographic areas. South-eastern Australia, Southern Australia.
