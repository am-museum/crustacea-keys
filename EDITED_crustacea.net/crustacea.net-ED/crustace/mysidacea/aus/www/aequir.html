Siriella aequiremis Hansen, 1910

Siriella aequiremis Hansen, 1910: 40, pl. III, fig. 4a-c, pl. IV, fig. 1a-l. --Tattersall, 1911: 122. --Hansen, 1912: 194. --Colosi, 1919: 6. --Colosi, 1920: 236, pl. 18, fig. 19. --Illig, 1930: 562. --Coifmann, 1937: 18, pl. VI, figs 10a-b. --Coifmann, 1937: 3. --Tattersall, 1943: 65. --Tattersall, 1951: 78). --Tattersall, 1955: 86. -- Gordan, 1957: 377. --Ii, 1964: 135, figs 36. --Pillai, 1965: 1692, fig. 17. --Pillai, 1973: 47, fig. 15, 16. --Mauchline & Murano, 1977: 76. --Murano, 1983: 82. --Fenton, 1985c: 37, 42 (key). --Carleton & Hamner, 1989: 464, table 1. --Muller, 1993: 32. --Panampunnayil, 1995: 1945 (table). --Fukoka & Murano, 2002: 98 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 465.

Type material. SYNTYPES: 6 specimens, ZMC, many specimenns, ZMA.

Type locality. Indonesia (8°0.3'S 116°59'E); between Bahuluwang and Tambolungan Islands, south of Saleyer, 8–10 m depth; Pulau Sebangkaran, Borneo, 39 m depth; south-eastern side of Pearl Bank, Sulu Archipelago, 15 m depth; North-Ubian, 16–23 m (6°7.5'N 120°26'E); near Celebes (1°16.5'S 130°15.8'E); the Moluccas (2°30'S 129°28'E); north of New Guinea (1°53.5'S 126°39'E) and (4°16'S 130°15.8'E, 4956 m depth).

Description. Based on Hansen, 1910: 40, pl. III, fig. 4a-c, pl. IV, fig. 1a-l.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins without setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine present, apical suture present. Labrum with medial process.
Pleon. Pleopod 2-4 biramous. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami straight. Pleopod 3 endopod subequal to exopod. Pleopod 4 endopod with multiple articles, longer than exopod; exopod with 10 articles, second last article subequal to third last article. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present on entire medial margin, slender setae present, endopod and exopod subequal in length; exopod diaresis present, 8–12 robust setae present, slender setae present below diaresis. Telson longer than broad, distally rounded, entire, without plumose setae, robust setae present along entire outer margins.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami absent.

Habitat. Coral reef, pelagic.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Davies Reef, Great Barrier Reef (Carleton & Hamner, 1989). Western Australia: west coast (Pillai, 1973).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia; South-western Australia.

Extrinsic distribution. Indo West Pacific: Red Sea (Coifman, 1936); Indian Ocean (Pillai, 1973); Philippine Islands (Tattersall, 1951); South China Sea (Ii, 1964).
