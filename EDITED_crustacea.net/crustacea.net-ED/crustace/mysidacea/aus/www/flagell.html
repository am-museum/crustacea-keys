Haplostylus flagelliforma Panampunnayil, 1997

Haplostylus flagelliforma Panampunnayil, 1997: 1205, figs 1–4. --Hanamura, 1998: 166 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 435.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: 1 male, 7 mm, IOBC-0494–10–50–1996. PARATYPES: 1 female, IOBC-0494 A-10–50–1996; 5 males, 5 females, IOBC-0494 B-10–50–1996.

Type locality. Off south-western Australian coast from Busselton to Albany (33° - 35°S to 114° - 119°E).

Description. Based on Panampunnayil, 1997: 1205, figs 1–4.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, broadly rounded; spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale ratio of length x width 2:1 to 5:1, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins without setae, spines on lateral margins absent, spines on medial margin absent, distally rounded, distal spine present, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner absent. Labrum with medial process; medial process without setae.
Pereon. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopod 1 biramous. Pleopod 2 biramous. Pleopod 4 biramous, endopod with a single article, endopod shorter than exopod, exopod with 1 articles, exopod second last article third last article absent. Uropod endopod diaresis present, robust setae present, 6–8 robust setae from statocyst nearly to the apex, slender setae present, endopod and exopod subequal in length. Uropod exopod diaresis present, 12–14 robust setae present, slender setae on lateral margin absent. Telson longer than broad, distally acute or subacute, cleft or notched, cleftness less than 20%, without plumose setae, robust setae on inner margin present, robust setae along entire inner margin, robust setae on lateral margins present, on entire lateral margins.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopod 2 uniramous or rudimentary, pseudobranchial rami absent. Pleopod 4 uniramous or greatly reduced.

Habitat. Planktonic.

Depth zones. Littoral.

Australian distribution. Western Australia: off south-western coast from Busselton to Albany (33° - 35°S to 114° - 119°E).

Australian geographic areas. South-western Australia.
