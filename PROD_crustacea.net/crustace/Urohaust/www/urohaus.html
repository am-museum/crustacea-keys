Urohaustorius Sheard, 1936

Urohaustorius Sheard, 1936: 445. –Barnard & Drummond, 1982: 47.

Type species. Urohaustorius halei Sheard, 1936, by original designation.

Description. Maxilla 1 palp with some setae displaced subapically.
Gnathopod 1 simple; coxa small, subequal in size to coxa 2 or slightly larger than coxa 2. Gnathopod 2 minutely parachelate or minutely chelate. Pereopod 3 coxa large, larger than coxa 4. Pereopod 4 coxa large. Pereopod 5 dactylus with robust setae, some robust setae in pairs or robust setae in single row. Pereopod 7 coxa without long posterior spine; basis fully expanded.
Uropod 1 inner ramus not fused to peduncle. Uropod 2 inner ramus not fused to peduncle. Telson moderately cleft or weakly cleft or emarginate or entire.
