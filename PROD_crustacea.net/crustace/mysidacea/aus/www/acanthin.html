Doxomysis acanthina Talbot, 1997

Doxomysis acanthina Talbot, 1997: 428, figs 1–3. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 451.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, AM P42693. PARATYPES: 1 female, AM P42694; 2 males, AM P43138; 3 males USNM 259765; 1 female, USNM 259766.

Type locality. Lizard Island lagoon, Queensland, Australia.

Description. Based on Talbot, 1997: 428, figs 1–3.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, narrowly rounded, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, longer than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins with setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner present. Labrum symmetrical.
Pereon. Body with moderate spination. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with distinct carpus and propodus, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-4 biramous. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami straight. Pleopod 4 endopod with multiple articles, endopod shorter than exopod; exopod with 7 articles, second last article longer than third last article, second last article slightly longer than third last article. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, 22–23 setae from statocyst nearly to the apex of medial margin, plumose setae present, exopod moderately longer than endopod; exopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae absent on lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally rounded, cleft or notched 20–30%, with two plumose setae, robust setae present along entire inner and outer margins.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami absent.

Habitat. Pelagic or associated with Porites coral.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Lizard Island lagoon, Great Barrier Reef (Talbot, 1997)

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.
