Australomysis aseta (Bacescu & Udrescu, 1982)

Tenagomysis aseta Bacescu & Udrescu, 1982: 89, fig. 4.
Australomysis aseta. --Fenton, 1991b: 334. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 451.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, QM W11248. PARATYPES: 1 female, QM W8517; 1 female, MGAB No. 582.

Type locality. Middle Banks, Moreton Bay, Queensland, Australia (27°11'S 153°21'E), 13 m depth.

Description. Based on Bacescu & Udrescu, 1982: 89, fig. 4.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, rounded, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale longer than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins with setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner absent. Labrum with medial process; medial process without setae.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-4 biramous. Pleopod 4 biramous, endopod with multiple articles, subequal to or longer than exopod. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, 32 setae from statocyst nearly to the apex of medial margin, exopod moderately longer than endopod; exopod diaresis absent. Telson longer than broad, distally truncate, cleft or notched 20–30%, without plumose setae, robust setae present on proximal three quarters of inner margin, along entire outer margins.
Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopods 1-4 uniramous or rudimentary.  Pleopod 4 uniramous or rudimentary. Uropod endopod with 34 setae from statocyst nearly to the apex of medial margin.

Habitat. Continental shelf, soft bottom.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Middle Banks, Moreton Bay (QM).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.
