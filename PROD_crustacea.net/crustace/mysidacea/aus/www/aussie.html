Halemysis australiensis Bacescu & Udrescu, 1984

Halemysis australiensis Bacescu & Udrescu, 1984: 94, figs 1–2. --Fenton, 1985c: 124, 129 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 459.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, MGAB no. 585. PARATYPES: 1 male QM, 1 female, MGAB no. 586, 4 females, 1 juvenile, MGAB no. 586.

Type locality. Memory Cave, Dangerous Reef, Port Lincoln, South Australia, 9.6 m depth.

Description. Based on Bacescu & Udrescu, 1984: 94, figs 1–2.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, broadly rounded, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, lateral margins with setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner present. Labrum symmetrical.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-4 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 4 endopod with multiple articles. Uropod endopod robust setae absent. Telson longer than broad, distally rounded, entire, without plumose setae, robust setae present along distal two thirds of lateral margin.

Habitat. Rock reef, sublittoral; phototrophic.

Australian distribution. South Australia: Port Lincoln (Bacescu & Udrescu, 1984); Horseshoe Reef, Christies Beach (Fenton, 1985c).

Australian geographic areas. Southern Australia.
