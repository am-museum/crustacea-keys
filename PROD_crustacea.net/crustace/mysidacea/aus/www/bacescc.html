Siriella bacescui Udrescu, 1981

Siriella bacescui Udrescu, 1981: 29, fig. 1a-k. --Fenton, 1985c: 37, 46 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003:  466.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, MGAB No. 577. PARATYPES: 1 female, MGAB No. 578, 5 females, MGAB No. 579.

Type locality. Bennett Island, Chesterfield Islands, Coral Sea, 15 m depth.

Description. Based on Udrescu, 1981: 29, fig. 1a-k.
Head. Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins without setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine present, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner absent.
Pereon. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-4 biramous. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami spirally coiled. Pleopod 3 endopod shorter than exopod. Pleopod 4 endopod shorter than exopod. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, 45–48 setae from statocyst nearly to the apex of medial margin, slender setae present, endopod and exopod subequal in length; exopod diaresis present, 4 robust setae present, slender setae present, on distal quarter of lateral martgin. Telson longer than broad, distally rounded, entire, with two plumose setae, robust setae present along entire outer margins with a median gap.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami absent.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Bennett Island, Chesterfield Islands, Coral Sea (Bacescu, 1981).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.
