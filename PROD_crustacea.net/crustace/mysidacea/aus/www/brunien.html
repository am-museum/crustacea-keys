Tenagomysis bruniensis Fenton, 1991

Tenagomysis sp. 3. --Fenton, 1985c: 113, 120 (key).
Tenagomysis bruniensis Fenton, 1991b: 332: figs 19–26. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 455.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, TMH G3320. PARATYPES: 1 female, 1 male, TMH G3321; 2 females, 5 males, TMH G3322.

Type locality. Moorina Bay, Bruny Island, Tasmania, Australia (43º14´S 147º23´E), 3 m depth.

Description. Based on Fenton, 1991b: 332, figs 19–26.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 10:1 to 15:1 x width, longer than antenna 1 peduncle, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present. Labrum symmetrical.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided, terminal brush of setae present.
Pleon. Pleopod 1 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopods 2-4 biramous. Pleopod 4 endopod with multiple articles, endopod shorter than exopod; exopod with 9 articles, second last article longer than third last article, second last article more than double the length shorter. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, 40 setae on entire medial margin, slender setae present, exopod much longer than endopod; exopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae present, below diaresis. Telson longer than broad, distally angled and convex, cleft or notched 20–30%, with two plumose setae, robust setae present along entire inner and outer margins.

Habitat. Benthic, sublittoral; soft bottom, planktonic.

Australian distribution. Tasmania: Moorina Bay, Bruny Island; Hope Beach, South Arm; Little Swanport Bay, East coast (Fenton, 1991b).

Australian geographic areas. Southern Australia.
