Gastrosaccus daviei Bacesscu & Udrescu, 1982

Gastrosaccus daviei Bacesscu & Udrescu, 1982: 80–83, fig. 1a-r. --Fenton, 1985c: 56, 60 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 434.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, QM W9758 (not QM W8657 as published). PARATYPES: QM W9759, QM W8515-W518, MGAB No. 583, 2 specimens.

Type locality. Middle Banks, Moreton Bay, Queensland (27°11'S 153°21'E), 11–15 m depth.

Description. Based on Bacesscu & Udrescu, 1982: 80–83, fig. 1A-R.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins without setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine present, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner present. Labrum with medial process; medial process without setae.
Pereon. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided, terminal brush of setae present.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-4 biramous. Pleopod 3 endopod longer than exopod. Pleopod 4 endopod with a single article, endopod shorter than exopod; exopod with 7 articles, second last article subequal to third last article. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, 18–20 setae from statocyst nearly to the apex of medial margin, slender setae present, endopod and exopod subequal in length; exopod diaresis absent, 14–15 robust setae present, slender setae absent on lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally truncate, cleft or notched 20–30%, without plumose setae, robust setae present on the proximal quarter of inner margin only, along entire outer margins.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopods 1-4 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami triangular.

Habitat. Sandy bottom.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Bramble Bay, Middle Banks, Moreton Bay (Bacescu & Udrescu, 1982).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.
