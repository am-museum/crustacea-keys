Euchaetomeropsis merolepis (Illig, 1908)

Euchaetomera merolepis Illig, 1908: 550, figs 1–3.
Euchaetomera tenuis. --Lo Bianco, 1903: 191 (in part).
Euchaetomeropsis merolepis. --Tattersall, 1909: 130. --Colosi, 1922: 417. --Zimmer, 1914: 395. --Colosi, 1929: 417. --Illig, 1930: 450, figs 97–103. --Nouvel, 1943: 82–83. --Tattersall, 1943: 67. --Tattersall, 1955: 133. --Gordan, 1957: 349. -- Casanova, 1968: 153, figs 1–6. --Murano, 1977: 150, fig 5. -- Taniguchi, 1974: 355, fig. 3. --Mauchline & Murano, 1977: 55. --Mauchline, 1980: 328. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 444.
Euchaetomeropsis merolepsis. --Fenton, 1985c: 75.

Type material. PARATYPES: BMNH, ZMB cat. no. 22 591.

Type locality. South of the Cocos (Keeling) Islands, Indian Ocean.

Description. Based on Illig, 1908: 550, figs 1–3.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 6.1 to 9:1 x width, lateral margins with setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner absent.
Pereon. Body smooth.
Pleon. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present from statocyst nearly to the apex of medial margin, slender setae present, exopod moderately longer then endopod; exopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae present, on entire lateral margin. Telson about as long as broad, distally truncate, entire, without plumose setae, robust setae absent.

Habitat. Mesopelagic.

Australian distribution. Western Australia: west coast  (Taniguchi, 1974).

Extrinsic distribution. Indian Ocean (Illig, 1908; Illig, 1930; Taniguchi, 1974). Pacific Ocean (Tattersall, 1943; Murano, 1977). Atlantic Ocean (Zimmer, 1914; Illig, 1930; Tattersall, 1955).
