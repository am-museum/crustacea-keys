Anisomysis mullini Murano, 1987

Anisomysis mullini Murano, 1987: 47, figs 1–3. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 458.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, NSMT Cr9012.

Type locality. Fore reef of Davies Reef (east of Townsville), Great Barrier Reef, Queensland, Australia, 2–3 m depth.

Description. Based on Murano, 1987: 47, figs 1–3.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, broadly rounded, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 6.1 to 9:1 x width, longer than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins with setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture absent, spine on outer distal corner absent. Labrum symmetrical.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, shorter than perepod 4, endopod slender, with distinct carpus and propodus, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopod 4 biramous; endopod with a single article, endopod shorter than exopod; exopod with 4 articles, second last article longer than third last article, second last article more than double the length shorter. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae present, exopod moderately longer than endopod; exopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae present, on entire lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally concave, entire, without plumose setae, robust setae present along distal half of outer margin.

Habitat. Coral reef, sublittoral.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Davies Reef, Great Barrier Reef (Murano, 1987).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.
