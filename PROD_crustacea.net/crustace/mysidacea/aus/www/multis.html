Haplostylus multispinosus Panampunnayil, 1997

Haplostylus multispinosus Panampunnayil, 1997: 1217, figs 9–12. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 436.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: 1 male, 8 mm, IOBC-0496–10–50–1996. PARATYPES: 1 female, IOBC-0496 A-10–50–1996; 5 males, 5 females, IOBC-0496 B-10–50–1996.

Type locality. Off Western Australian coast between Busselton and Albany, Australia (33° - 35° S to 114° - 119°E).

Description. Based on Panampunnayil, 1997: 1217, figs 9–12.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, narrowly rounded, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins without setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine present, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner absent. Labrum with medial process; medial process without setae.
Pereon. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided, terminal brush of setae present.
Pleon. Pleopods1-4 biramous. Pleopod 3 endopod shorter than exopod. Pleopod 4 endopod with a single article; exopod with 1 articles, second last article pleopod reduced. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, 13 setae from statocyst nearly to the apex of medial margin, slender setae present or plumose setae present, endopod and exopod subequal in length; exopod diaresis absent, 12–13 robust setae present, slender setae absent on lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally angled and convex, cleft or notched 20–30%, without plumose setae, robust setae present along entire inner and outer margins.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopod 2 uniramous or rudimentary.

Habitat. Planktonic.

Depth zones. Littoral.

Australian distribution. Western Australia: south-west coast between Busselton and Albany (Panampunnayil, 1997).

Australian geographic areas. South-western Australia.
