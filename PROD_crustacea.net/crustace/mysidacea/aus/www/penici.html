Anchialina penicillata Zimmer, 1915

Anchialina penicillata Zimmer, 1915: 161, figs 7–12. --Tattersall, 1940: 331. --Tattersall, 1951: 103. --Gordan, 1957: 339. --Tattersall, 1960: 176. --Ii, 1964: 201. --Pillai, 1965: 1702, figs. 39–41. --Tattersall, 1940: 331. --Mauchline & Murano, 1977, tab. 1. --Fenton, 1985c: 54 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 432.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: ZMH.

Type locality. Between Sri Lanka and Dampier Straits, New Guinea.

Description. Based on Zimmer, 1915: 161, figs 7–12.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins without setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine present, apical suture absent, spine on outer distal corner absent.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod.
Pleon. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, on entire medial margin, slender setae present, endopod moderately longer than exopod subequal; exopod diaresis absent, 13 robust setae present, slender setae absent on lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally acute or subacute, cleft or notched 20–30%, without plumose setae, robust setae present along entire inner margin, along distal two thirds of lateral margin.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Great Barrier Reef (Lowry & Stoddart, 2003). New South Wales: Port Stephens (Tattersall, 1940).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia, South-eastern Australia.

Extrinsic distribution. Between Sri Lanka and Dampier Straits, New Guinea (Zimmer, 1915); Marshall Islands and Philippine Islands (Tattersall, 1951).
