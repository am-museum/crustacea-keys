Australomysis reubeni Panampunnayil, 1992

Australomysis reubeni Panampunnayil, 1992: 549, 553, figs 18–38. --Lowry & Stoddart, 451.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: female, 4.8 mm, IOBC 0399–10–50–1989. PARATYPE: 1 male, 4.2 mm, IOBC 0399A-10–50–1989.

Type locality. Off south-western coast of Australia (33º42'S 114º40'E).

Description. Based on Panampunnayil, 1992: 549, 553, figs 18–38.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, sub-acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, longer than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins with setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture absent, spine on outer distal corner present. Labrum symmetrical.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, shorter than perepod 4, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-4 biramous. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami straight. Pleopod 3 endopod subequal to exopod. Pleopod 4 endopod with multiple articles, endopod shorter than exopod; exopod with 10 articles, second last article subequal to third last article. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present, 24 setae from statocyst nearly to the apex, slender setae present, exopod moderately longer then endopod; exopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae present, on entire lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally truncate, cleft or notched 20–30%, without plumose setae, robust setae present along entire inner and outer margins.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopods 1-4 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami triangular. Pleopod 4 endopod with a single article.

Habitat. Pelagic.

Australian distribution. Western Australia: off south-western coast (Panumpunnayil, 1992).

Australian geographic areas. South-western Australia.
