Anisomysis robustispina Panampunnayil, 1984

Anisomysis robustispina Panampunnayil, 1984: 948, figs 19–39. --Fenton, 1985c: 124, 129 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 459.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: male, IOBC 0385–10–50–1983. PARATYPES: 1 female, IOBC 0385A-10–50–1983; 3 females, 3 males IOBC 0385B-10–50–1983.

Type locality. Off south-western coast of Australia between Busselton and Point Hood (33º14´S - 35º16´S 114º28´E - 119º29´E).

Description. Based on Panampunnayil, 1984: 948, figs 19–39.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, narrowly rounded, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 6.1 to 9:1 x width, longer than or subequal in length to antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins with setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner absent. Labrum symmetrical.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, subequal in length to pereopod 4, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-3 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 4 biramous; endopod with a single article, endopod shorter than exopod; exopod with 4 articles, second last article longer than third last article, second last article slightly longer than third last article. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae present, exopod moderately longer than endopod; exopod diaresis absent, robust setae absent, slender setae absent on lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally concave, emarginate, without plumose setae, robust setae present along distal half of outer margin.

Habitat. Continental shelf, pelagic.
Australian distribution. Western Australia: off south-western coast between Busselton and Point Hood (Panampunnayil, 1984.

Australian geographic areas. South-western Australia.
