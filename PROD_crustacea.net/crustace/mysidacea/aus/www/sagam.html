Acanthomysis sagamiensis (Nakazawa, 1910)

Metamysis sagamiensis Nakazawa, 1910: 251, pl.8, fig. 32.
Neomysis sagamiensis. --Illig, 1930: 597 (key). --Tattersall, 1932: 317 (key). --Gordan, 1597: 369.
Acanthomysis sagamiensis. --Ii, 1936: 589. --Banner, 1948: 86. --Ii, 1964: 488–489. --Mauchline & Murano, 1977: 45, table 1. --Mauchline, 1980: 336. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 456.

Type material. Whereabouts unknown (believed lost).

Type locality. Enoshima in Sagami Bay, Japan.

Description. Based on Nakazawa, 1910: 251, pl.8, fig. 32.
Head. Rostrum present, triangular, acute. Antenna 2 scale longer than antenna 1 peduncle.
Pleon. Uropod endopod 7 robust setae present, in statocyst region only; robust setae absent, slender setae present, on entire lateral margin. Telson longer than broad, distally rounded, entire, without plumose setae, robust setae present along entire outer margins with a distal gap.

Habitat. Sublittoral.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Great Barrier Reef (AM).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.

Extrinsic distribution. Japan (Nakazawa, 1910).
