Gastrosaccus sarae Panampunnayil, 1999

Gastrosaccus sarae Panampunnayil, 1999: 692: figs 40–73.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: adult male, IOBC-0500–10–50–1998. ALLOTYPE: adult female, IOBC-0500A-10–50–1998. PARATYPES: 5 males, 5 females IOBC 1500B-10–50–1998.

Type locality. Indian Ocean between 33°18'-34°52' S and 115°01'-115°58' E.

Description. Based on Panampunnayil, 1999: 692: figs 40–73. Head stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions; spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, rostrum broadly rounded. Antenna 2 ratio of length x width 2:1 to 5:1, scale shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, scale lateral margins without setae. Antenna 2 scale spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale spines on medial margin absent. Antenna 2 scale distal spine present, apical suture present, distally rounded, spine on outer distal corner absent. Labrum with medial process. Medial process without setae. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, endopod carpus/propodus endopod with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided. Pereopod 3 terminal brush of setae absent. Pleopod 1 biramous. Pleopod 2 biramous. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami straight. Pleopod 3 biramous. Pleopod 3 shorter than exopod. Pleopod 4 biramous, endopod with a single article, endopod shorter than exopod; exopod 7 articles, exopod second last article subequal to third last article. Uropod endopod diaresis absent; endopod and exopod subequal in length; robust setae present, robust setae from statocyst nearly to the apex. Uropod endopod 13. Uropod endopod slender setae present; exopod diaresis absent, exopod present. Uropod exopod 16. Uropod exopod absent. Telson longer than broad, cleft or notched, 20–30%, distally rounded, without plumose setae, telson, robust setae on inner margin present, along entire inner margin, robust setae on lateral margins present, entire lateral margins.

Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopod 1 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 2 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami absent. Pleopod 3 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 4 uniramous or greatly reduced.

Habitat. Planktonic.

Depth zones. Littoral.

Australian distribution. Western Australia.

Australian geographic areas. South-western Australia.
