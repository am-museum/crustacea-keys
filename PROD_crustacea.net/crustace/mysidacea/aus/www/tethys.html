Heteromysis (Heteromysis) tethysiana Bacescu, 1983

Heteromysis tethysiana Bacescu, 1983: 6, fig. 2. --Fenton, 1985c: 142, 146 (key).
Heteromysis (Heteromysis) tethysiana. --Murano, 1988: 48 (key). --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 448.

Type material. HOLOTYPE: female, QM W9688. PARATYPES: 1 specimen, QM W9689; 1 specimen, MGAB no. 594.

Type locality. Heron Island, Queensland, Australia (23°27'S 151°55'E).

Description. Based on Bacescu, 1983: 6–11, fig. 2.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, narrowly rounded, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine absent, apical suture present. Labrum symmetrical.
Pereon. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with carpus and propodus fused and subdivided, terminal brush of setae absent.
Pleon. Pleopods 1-4 uniramous or rudimentary. Pleopod 4 endopod with a single article. Uropod endopod robust setae absent. Telson longer than broad, distally truncate, cleft or notched 20–30%, without plumose setae, robust setae present along entire inner margin, along distal half of outer margin.

Habitat. Coral reefs.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Heron Island, Great Barrier Reef (Bacescu, 1983). New South Wales: Middleton Reef, Tasman Sea (AM).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.
