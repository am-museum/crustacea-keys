Type material. HOLOTYPE: probable, whereabouts unknown (believed lost).

Type locality. Atlantic Ocean between Madeira and the West Indies.

Description. Based on Milne Edwards,1837: 462.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions, spine at anterodistal corner of eye stalk absent; rostrum present, triangular, acute, spines on lateral margins absent. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, shorter than antenna 1 peduncle, lateral margins without setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine present, apical suture present, spine on outer distal corner absent.
Pereon. Body smooth. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, terminal brush of setae present.
Pleon. Pleopods 2-4 biramous. Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami spirally coiled. Pleopod 3 endopod subequal to exopod. Pleopod 4 endopod with multiple articles. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present on entire medial margin, slender setae present, exopod moderately longer then endopod; exopod diaresis present, 2–5, robust setae present, slender setae present on distal quarter of lateral margin, below diaresis. Telson longer than broad, distally rounded, entire, with two plumose setae, robust setae present along entire outer margins.
Female (sexually dimorphic characters). Pleopod 2 pseudobranchial rami triangular.
Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.

Habitat. Planktonic.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Davies Reef, Great Barrier Reef (Carleton & Hamner, 1989); Low Isles, Great Barrier Reef (Tattersall, 1936a). New South Wales: coastal Eddy J (McWilliam & Phillips, 1983). Western Australia: west coast (Pillai, 1973). Australian waters between Sydney, Australia and Auckland, New Zealand (Sars, 1885).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia, South-eastern Australia, Southern Australia.

Extrinsic distribution. Indian Ocean: Red Sea and Indian Ocean (Czerniavsky, 1882; Illig, 1930; Coifman, 1936). Pacific Ocean: south of Kingsmills, near Sherson Island (Dana, 1852); Hawaii (Ortmann, 1905; Hansen, 1912). East of the Society Islands, South Pacific Ocean (Dana, 1852); east and north-east of Japan (Ii, 1964); off Chilli (Sars, 1885). Atlantic Ocean between Madeira and the West Indies (Milne Edwards,1837); off Cape Verde (Sars, 1885).
