Anchialina zimmeri Tattersall, 1951

Anchialina zimmeri Tattersall, 1951: 103, fig. 31. --Gordan, 1957: 340. --Ii, 1964: 201. --Mauchline & Murano, 1977: 47, tab. 1. Bacescu, 1979: 143. --Fenton, 1985c: 54, 55 (key). --Fukuoka & Murano, 1997: 523, fig. 3F, G. --Lowry & Stoddart, 2003: 433.
Anchialina zimmer. --Wang & Liu, 1987: 224, fig. 10.

Type material. SYNTYPES: 17 males, 5 females, USNM 00081258.

Type locality. Sablayan Bay Anchorage, Mindano Strait, Philippine Islands.

Description. Based on Tattersall, 1951: 103, fig, 31.
Head. Eyes stalked, with visual elements, ommatidia not divided into separate regions; rostrum present, triangular, acute. Antenna 2 scale length 2:1 to 5:1 x width, lateral margins without setae, without spines, medial margins without spines, distal margin rounded, distal spine present, apical suture absent, spine on outer distal corner present.
Pereon. Pereopod 3 forming a pereopod, endopod slender, with distinct carpus and propodus, terminal brush of setae present.
Pleon. Pleopod 3 biramous; endopod shorter than exopod. Uropod endopod diaresis absent, robust setae present on entire medial margin, slender setae present, endopod and exopod subequal in length; exopod diaresis absent, robust setae present. Telson longer than broad, distally acute or subacute, cleft or notched 20–30%, without plumose setae, robust setae present along entire inner margin, along distal two thirds of lateral margin.

Habitat. Planktonic.

Australian distribution. Queensland: Heron Island, Great Barrier Reef (Bacescu, 1979).

Australian geographic areas. North-eastern Australia.

Extrinsic distribution. South China Sea (Wang & Liu, 1987). Sabalayan Bay, Mindanao Strait, Philippine Islands (Tattersall, 1951); Ryukyu Islands, Japan (Fukuoka & Murano, 1997).
